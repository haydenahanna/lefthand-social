About

The Story Behind the Brand

Hi, I'm Hayden

I'm a marketing strategist and content creator who believes in building brands that genuinely connect with people.

After years of working with businesses across different industries, from luxury real estate to island resorts to local restaurants, I've learned that great marketing isn't about following trends or gaming algorithms. It's about understanding what makes your brand unique and communicating that in a way that resonates.

I started Lefthand Social because I wanted to work closely with brands I believe in, creating content that feels authentic rather than generic. Every business has a story worth telling, and I'm here to help you tell it.

My Approach

Why "Lefthand"?

Sometimes the best approach is the unexpected one.

In a world where marketing can feel repetitive and predictable, I believe in finding the angle that others miss. The name Lefthand Social represents that different perspective, the willingness to try something new while staying true to what works.

It's also a nod to thinking with both sides of the brain: the creative and the strategic, the artistic and the analytical. Great marketing requires both.
