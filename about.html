The Story Behind the Brand

Our Story

Meet Hayden, Founder Boulder, Colorado & beyond

Lefthand Social started with a simple realization: too many great businesses go unseen, not because they aren't good enough, but because the right people never find them.

74% of people research a brand on social media before they ever buy.

Often, the only difference between a business that thrives and one that struggles is whether people can find it. That's why we exist. We help brands show up authentically where their customers already are, turning attention into real connection and lasting growth.

Over the years, we've worked with businesses across a wide range of industries, from luxury real estate and boutique hospitality to restaurants, retail, and beyond. Different brands, different goals, but the same mission every time: helping them show up with confidence and reach the people who matter most.

Why Lefthand?

Your left hand is governed by the right side of your brain, the creative, intuitive, artistic side.

That's where we lead from. Strategy and analytics are the foundation of everything we do, but creativity is what makes a brand feel genuine, and genuine is what people remember. Most marketing leans on the analytical and forgets the art. We make room for both.

Lefthand Social is a reminder that the best marketing is strategic and creative at once: built on data, brought to life through storytelling and design.

It's also a nod to our founder, who's left-handed, and to the hands-on, personal approach that runs through everything we make. That human touch is what makes a brand feel genuine.
