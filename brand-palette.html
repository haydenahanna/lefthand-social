The Color System

One palette, rotated by season — always recognizably yours.

Two tiers. Neutrals (black, cream, stone) are the workhorses — text, backgrounds, lines — and carry ~90% of everything; they're the foundation, not "brand colors." On top sit your brand colors: Bordeaux as the signature you own year-round, plus three soft accents (powder pink, avocado, baby blue) that lean into different seasons while still reading as one brand. On the feed, let imagery (palms, ocean, food) bring the brightness; the palette keeps the brand consistent on top of it.
